Campus Trading Post FAQ

Disclaimer: This FAQ page is for illustrative purposes only and does not reflect the final website design. The content is subject to change without notice at any time.

The Campus Trading Post is an online platform for university students, faculty, and staff to buy, sell, and trade goods and services. It provides a secure and easy-to-use environment for campus transactions.

The Campus Trading Post is available to all enrolled students, faculty, and staff members of the university. You must log in with your university credentials to access the platform.

To post an item, log in to your account, go to the 'Post an Item' section, fill out the form with the item details (including price and category), and submit it. Your listing will be reviewed before being made publicly visible.

No, the Trading Post System is free to use for all university students, faculty, and staff. There are no fees for posting items or browsing listings.

You can contact a seller or buyer by using the 'Message' feature on their listing page. You will be able to send private messages to discuss further details and arrange transactions.

If you experience issues with a transaction, please contact the Trading Post support team immediately. We can assist in resolving disputes or offer guidance on how to proceed with the transaction safely.

To remove a listing, go to your 'My Posts' section, select the item you wish to remove, and click the 'Delete' button. The listing will be permanently removed from the platform.
